Shark Attacks in Florida (2015-16)

The state of Florida is known for its beaches -- and, often, its shark attacks. Check out the map above for the locations of every shark attack in Florida between 2015-16. Each of these attacks was labelled as "unprovoked," and none of them were fatal.

This map was constructed by Brittney Miller. The data about the shark attacks came from Shark Attack Data, which compiles its information from the Global Shark Attack File.
